Trapeze

This silhouette tends to have: Shoulders wider than the hips. The narrowest part of this body is the waist.

 Oval

It is a silhouette that blurs the figure of the body because it is wider right at the waist, making it stand out above the shoulders and hips.

 Inverted Triangle

The inverted triangle body has the shape of a "V", that is, it is characterized by having much broader shoulders than the hips and poorly defined curves.

 Triangle

People with a triangle body tend to have more hips than shoulders and chest. Voluminous hips, thighs and buttocks and less bust.

 Rectangle

It is characterized by having equally wide shoulders, waist and hips. The curves, therefore, are less pronounced in this type of figure, so the chest and butt area tend to look flat.
