Hourglass

It stands out for being a proportionate body type between shoulders and hips, in addition to having a very marked waist. It is also characterized by having large breasts and buttocks.

 Inverted Triangle

Virtually straight trunk, broad shoulders and back, poorly defined waist and narrow hips and buttocks, as well as thin legs.

 Oval

Women with this type of figure have volume in the chest and abdomen, aligned shoulders and hips, and a poorly defined waist, which is usually wider than the hips.

 Rectangle

The rectangular body is characterized by having little pronounced curves, a straight silhouette, shoulders similar in width to the hips, a small waist and very long legs.

 Triangle

The so-called triangular bodies are characterized by having the upper half of the body significantly narrower than the lower part, with the hip and buttock area in the foreground, where the curves are more pronounced.
